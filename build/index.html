ICOVoting

ICOVoting - a unique information and rating platform on ICO startups for crypto investors, experts, analysts, journalists and people involved into crypto activities from all over the world. The project team developed, implemented and successfully operates a unique system of open rating ICO projects through expert voting on the blockchain technology.

ICOVoting was founded in December 2017.

Why do we launch an ICO?

We want to make our project better and more useful for everyone. We are going to expand the existing services and add some more, so that more people can use ICOVoting in their work. We also want to launch an active promotion on the Internet and mass media.

What does our token give?

Our token gives an opportunity to purchase advertising on our website and on the partners' websites. Our token - an advertisment on our website and it can be exchanged for cryptocurrency at the prices of the advertising market. Our token allows you to invest in expensive advertising of tomorrow at very attractive prices of today. We develop the project with your help, increasing website traffic that leads to higher prices for advertising on ICOVoting.
